OUR COMPANY

COMPANY OVERVIEW

For more than 30 years, Marine Resources Development Corporation (MRDC), a carrageenan manufacturer, has been engaged in continuous innovation of blending and compounding food stabilizers and binders using Carrageenan as a base material. Blends are customized specifically to cater the Carrageenan demand of major manufacturers in the local market. With our products Ricovis and Ricogel Series, further enhancements can be applied to different food and non-food. Our company core value of quality is reflected and incorporated in our operations, products, and services, and is at par with international brands to provide our customers with only the best.

PROCUREMENT

Only the finest seaweeds that meet strict standards are used for production.

PREPARATION

With equal care for each batch, the selected raw materials are dried, cleaned, sorted and tested. After which they are thoroughly washed to completely remove dirt and impurities.

EXTRACTION AND REFINEMENT

Every step undergoes a very stringent and thorough process of quality control and produces the different grades of Carrageenan to meet the requirements of the most particular demands of customers.
