PRODUCT APPLICATIONS

Research in carrageenan for product applications has produced blend after blend, each suiting a particular need or preference. Today, a wide array of carrageenan blends exists for those specific requirements, but blend customization continues to develop as a result of the excellent collaboration between food and stabilizer industries.

MEAT

RICOGEL MP48 SERIES

  • High water binding capacity
  • Reduced purge and cooking loss
  • Better slice-ability
  • Freeze-thaw stable

JELLY

RICOGEL WD38 SERIES

  • Provides complete range of texture from soft to elastic brittle gels
  • Gels at room temperature, therefore refrigeration is not required
  • Contributes to gel clarity

FROZEN DESSERTS

RICOGEL IC28 SERIES

  • Contributes to the smooth texture and creamy mouth feel
  • Control ice crystal formation
  • Contributes to overrun, ease of extrusion and over-all body of finished product

DAIRY BEVERAGES

RICOGEL BG58 SERIES

  • Stabilizes cocoa suspension at very low concentrations
  • Impart homogeneous appearance & texture to chocolate milk
  • Provides excellent mouthfeel & flavor release

SAUCES/CHEESE/CONFECTIONERY

RICOGEL VS68 SERIES

  • Imparts body and consistency
  • Provides desirable texture and homogeneity
  • Exhibits cohesiveness and structural property

BEER

RICOVIS/RICOGEL GU SERIES

  • Improves wort, shelf life, and clarity in minimal production time and use of filter acid
  • Imparts efficient protein with fast precipitation and flocculation
  • Improves beer haze and stability

PET FOOD/AIRFRESHENERS

RICOGEL NF78 SERIES

  • Uniforms moisture throughout the can
  • Adds richness to the gravy
  • Maintains uniform suspension
  • Easy unmolding from the can

PHARMACEUTICALS

RICOGEL NF78 SERIES

  • Thickens and controls stability
  • Provides pH and Thermal stability
  • Serves as a stabilizer, texturizer, and binder

PURE CARRAGEENAN

RICOGEL NF78 SERIES

  • Serves as a stabilizer, texturizer, and binder
