PRODUCT APPLICATIONS

Research in carrageenan for product applications has produced blend after blend, each suiting a particular need or preference. Today, a wide array of carrageenan blends exists for those specific requirements, but blend customization continues to develop as a result of the excellent collaboration between food and stabilizer industries.

MEAT

RICOGEL MP48 SERIES

  • Reduces purge, induces freeze-thaw stability and improves yield.
  • Prevents drip-loss during transit and distribution.
  • Reduces cooking loss.

JELLY

RICOGEL WD38 SERIES

  • Provides highly elastic and very pliable resilient gels with chewy texture.
  • Allows easy demoulding.
  • Gives soft, transparent and elastic gels for cold & instant desserts.

ICE CREAM

RICOGEL IC28 SERIES

  • Induces fine ice crystals.
  • Develops melting resistance.
  • Contributes to the smooth texture and creamy mouthfeel.

DAIRY BEVERAGES

RICOGEL BG58 SERIES

  • Provides high viscosity and uniform distribution of suspended solids.
  • Gives good shear stability to prevent viscosity loss.

SAUCES

RICOGEL VS68 SERIES

  • Thickens and controls flowability.
  • Provides pH and thermal stability.
  • Suspends spices, herbs, and vegetable bits.

CHEESE

RICOGEL VS68 SERIES

  • Provides sliceability and grating property.
  • Imparts desirable texture, and homogeneity.

CONFECTIONERY

RICOGEL VS68 SERIES

  • Imparts body and consistency.
  • Exhibits smooth texture and glossy finish.
  • Provides cohesiveness and structural property.

BEER

RICOVIS GU98 SERIES

  • Improves wort, shelf life, and clarity in minimal production time and use of filter acid
  • Imparts efficient protein with fast precipitation and flocculation

PET FOOD

RICOGEL NF78 SERIES

  • Enhances water and meat particle binding.
  • Provides stability and texture.
  • Allows easy unmoulding from the can.

AIR-FRESHENER GEL

RICOGEL NF78 SERIES

  • Serves as primary gelling agent.
  • Promotes long lasting fragrance.

PHARMACEUTICALS

RICOGEL NF78 SERIES

  • Gives viscosity and body.
  • Provides soft, odorless and gelatin-free material for encapsulation

PURE CARRAGEENAN

RICOVIS/RICOGEL GU SERIES

  • Serves as a stabilizer, texturizer, and binder
